Trachtenberg Rodes & Friedberg

  • Client: Trachtenberg Rodes & Friedberg LLP
  • Launch progect

    • A new responsive, mobile-friendly website and branding for problem solvers who bring their sound instincts, work ethic, experience, judgment, and knowledge to bear on every client problem.

    Trachtenberg Rodes & Friedberg is a full-service business law firm with 30 years’ experience representing a diverse range of U.S. and international businesses and commercial interests, including banks, Fortune 500 companies, investment firms and funds, closely held companies and partnerships, and individuals confronted with challenging legal and business issues.

    The firm approached Good2bSocial with some ideas about what it wanted out of a new website—mainly, an updated, more premium web experience featuring a simpler, more unified design that was more manageable and in line with today's web standards.

    Good2bSocial designed and developed the site, working closely with Trachtenberg Rodes & Friedberg to ensure the final product was exceptional. Along the way, Good2bSocial identified and addressed several key areas for improvement on the new site:

    Design and Usability: the previous site was slow, outdated, and difficult to navigate with multiple menus and inconsistent design conventions. Importantly, Trachtenberg Rodes & Friedberg didn't feel that the site catered to their premium target market. Good2bSocial redesigned the site to be more interactive, dynamic, and immersive, featuring clean lines, prominent headlines, and clear site navigation.

    Search Engine Optimization: Old URLs were not optimized and the site structure had become sprawling and inefficient. The new site was developed on Wordpress CMS, improving the URL structure while optimizing and automating the SEO process
