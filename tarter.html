Tarter Krinsky Website Redesign

  • Client: Tarter Krinsky & Drogin LLP
  • Launch progect

    • A new look for a strategy & management consulting firm focused on legal indsutry.


    Tarter Krinsky & Drogin is one of the best law firms in New York City. Their old website however, wasn't doing anything for them, so they approached Good2bSocial to design a bigger and better website with a completely new look. We drew inspiration from the intense passion that Tarter Krinsky & Drogin has for their work and other ventures, like giving back to the community and supporting the arts. Each page offers a slideshow of the cases that the firm have previous work on and a section of their website is dedicated to helping train and develop their employees since professional growth is at the heart what the firm does.

    Each lawyer has depths of information about them as do the practice areas, including related articles, speeches, and much more. Potential clients can really get an idea of the firm as a whole and of each lawyer by navigating around the Tarter Krinsky & Drogin website. The firm has also shown a dedication to scaling the site out through blogging and utilizing social media.

    We were impressed with the dedication and drive Tarter Krinsky & Drogin brought to the table when creating the website and updating it to current web standards. Now clients have access to more information about the firm, their practice areas, and a number of helpful resources, all in an appealing and easily navigable new website.
